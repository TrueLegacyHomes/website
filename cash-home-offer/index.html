Frequently Asked Questions

Have more questions? Contact us — we're happy to help.

How does a cash home offer work?
A cash home offer means we're ready to purchase your home without relying on bank financing. This speeds up the process significantly and eliminates complications like appraisals and loan approvals. You fill out a short form, we visit the property, and we present a written offer within 24–48 hours.
Will I get a fair price for my home?
Yes. We evaluate your property's condition, location, and current market value to present a competitive offer. Our goal is to balance speed and fairness so you can make a confident decision. We're transparent about how we arrive at our number — no low-ball tactics.
How fast can I close?
Most sales close in as little as 7–14 days, depending on your schedule. You choose the timeline that works best for you. If you need more time — for an estate sale, a move, or just peace of mind — we can accommodate that too.
Do I need to make repairs or clean out the home?
No. We buy homes in any condition—as-is. Whether the house needs updates, has deferred maintenance, or is still full of belongings, we handle it after closing. You're welcome to take what you want and leave the rest.
Are there any fees, commissions, or closing costs?
No commissions, no closing costs, and no hidden fees. The offer you accept is the exact amount you receive at closing. We cover all standard closing costs so you keep more of your money.
