The Playbook After Passing

Losing a loved one is overwhelming. This guide helps you navigate the practical matters so you can focus on what matters most—family and healing.

Amidst the grief, managing logistics can feel impossible. This checklist breaks down the essential tasks into four manageable areas, so you don't have to figure it all out alone.

1. Securing the Home & Belongings

The safety and security of your loved one's home is a crucial first step.

2. Notifying the Right People

There are many people and organizations who need to be informed.

3. Managing Digital Accounts

Today's world requires managing digital footprints alongside physical belongings.

4. Gathering Important Documents

You'll need these documents for legal, financial, and administrative purposes.

You Don't Have to Do This Alone

By systematically addressing each area, you can reduce the stress of managing practical matters after your loved one passes. While this guide provides a structured approach, don't hesitate to seek professional advice or support when needed.

Remember: grief isn't linear, and neither is this process. Take it one step at a time, and be gentle with yourself.
